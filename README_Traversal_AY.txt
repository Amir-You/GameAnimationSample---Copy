The game is called Portal Quest (WIP) due to there being a portal at the start and at the end. The game is meant to make the player feel intrigued as they progress through a few different color-themed environments and puzzles in order to escape.

---
Known Problems/Issues:

Pause button doesn't correctly close on keyboard button press. Need to click on the on-screen buttons instead.

No fully or properly implemented health and damage system. (Would go from 20 to 0 and not go up. Opacity set to zero to hide it.)

Can't immediately walk past doors until they're done opening.

Exit portal audio is on repeat if you stay too long.

No audio plays on switches pressed or doors opening.

Can walk on the water outside. (I think this is fine for the purposes. Could be magic water lol.)

I wasn't aware of the scope and the compounding nature of the assignment when we started so it ended up as a bit of a simple level but I think it works and has a certain charm. The portal theme also works as it allows the developer to experiment with other environments in later levels.
